Pet Menu

The Pet Menu can be accessed by right-clicking on the Pet Menu item while holding it in your mainhand. This menu shows all your pets and a Pet Leveling Display that shows your leveling progress with the active pet. You can also spawn, despawn and convert your pets back to an item here.

You can add pets to your pet menu by right-clicking on while holding it in your mainhand.

The menu below is an example of how the Pet Menu looks in-game when having collected all pets. Hover over each pet to see its details. Click on it to view a more in-depth description about each perk and how it scales.
